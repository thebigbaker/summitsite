Our Process

Our design and development process is proven to provide dynamic, responsive websites that load fast and offer the latest functionality and interactivity to help your business grow its online presence.

Discover

This is the most important step as we gather information about your business and industry that will allow us to create a unique website that will best fit the needs of your business. Whether through face-to-face meetings, phone conversations or email correspondence, we take your input and ideas and implement them into a functioning, responsive website.

Creative Design

Next we take your input and create basic wireframe designs showing you a mockup of what your website will look like. You will be able to see where and how content such as navigation, headers, widgets and other page elements will be positioned on your site. We also will assist in writing any content, such as an about description of your company, description of services your business provides, etc...

Development

Using the latest technologies, we build and code your custom website from scratch. Your new website is guaranteed to be responsive, interactive and 100% bug free. During this process we initialize and perform several tests during each phase of development to ensure your website looks and performs exactly to your specifications.

Deployment

It's now time to launch your new website! Although we currently do not provide hosting services, depending on the size and scope of your new website, we will work with you and recommend trusted hosting sites we prefer and have worked with ourselves. We will connect your domain to your new host, perform any needed server configurations, upload all files associated with your new website and finally, set your website to go live.
